Biz-Plan

Description

An opportunity to buckle young entrepreneurs with wise business ideas to stand a chance to get well rewarded. Dedicated to spark the spirit of entrepreneurship and pave the way from an idea to professional success, this event will provide a platform to showcase your witty impromptu thinking and your budding startup ideas in front of an esteemed panel of investors and dazzle the audience.

Category: Business Model Making Competition
Registration Fee: INR 150 (Team)

Rule

  • The competition is open to everyone. The teams must apply with their pitch deck.
  • A team can consist of a maximum of 5 members. Individual participation is also accepted. Only one of the team members should register as Team Leader.
  • One participant cannot be a part of multiple teams
  • The team structure is inflexible once the registration is complete.
  • Each team will be allotted 5 minutes (4 minutes for explaining their ideas and 1 minute for Q&A round).
  • Top three teams will get a chance to be mentored by renowned investors and entrepreneurs and the winner will also get investment in their ideas allocated by the investors.

Case Study

Description

In a case challenge, participants strive to develop the best solution to a real business case study within an allocated time frame, typically with teams of three to five individuals pitted against each other in a head-to-head or broader relative ranking. Teams deliver presentations for judges and, while competitions vary in composition, a standard format and purpose exists. It challenges the students from all across the nation to compete against each other and prove their business and entrepreneurial mettle in a real-life scenario. The participants are expected not only to have strong business skills but also the ability to identify entrepreneurship opportunities.

Category: Business Model Making Competition
Registration Fee: INR 150 (Team)

Rule

  • The initial round will be an online submission round in which participants will have to submit an initial draft of their solution consisting of not more than 450-500 words.
  • Participants also have to submit a presentation (PPT or PDF Format) on their solution.
  • 2 or 3 teams will be shortlisted and awarded.
  • The case statement will be released one week before the competition.

Stockify

Description

The Stock Market Challenge is an online simulation trading game where you create and manage your own portfolio and compete with other players in a risk-free environment. Access financial information on stocks to get a better insight into their potential and fundamentals.

Category: Virtual Trading
Registration Fee: Free

Rule

  • The event will be live from 2 weeks before the E-Summit.
  • The existing dummy cash balance will be cleared and an amount of Rs. 10 lakh dummy cash will be credited to the account of each participant.
  • They can trade according to BSE.
  • A specific time slot will be mentioned where each participant has to report and show us their final account balance. They must update their final net-worth to our assigned volunteer’s datasheet.
  • After submitting the details or after the given timeslot, no participant will be allowed to enter any data regarding their final net worth.
  • Winners will be decided by calculating the highest net-worth among the participants. The prize will be given to the top 3.

Description

Curious about how well you know business and technology-related topics? Brain-It-Out is an online quiz competition on business, technology, and innovation. It's time for your adrenaline to start flowing. This is a solo event where each participant will be quizzed on relevant business topics and market scenarios.

Category: Business Quiz
Registration Fee: INR 70 (Individual)

Rule

  • It is a solo event.
  • 1 round of quiz will be held on 4th June at 3:30 PM and then the winners will be announced.
  • The link to the video conference will be emailed to the participants by 3rd June, 11:59 PM.
  • The remaining rules and the event structure will be updated soon.
